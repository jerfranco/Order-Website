Welcome to Oompa Lumpia

Order your favorite Filipino food here!

Adobo

Uses a cooking method indigenous to the Philippines, which often involves preserving food with vinegar and salt in a tropical climate.

Sisig

A famous dish that combines tender and crisp meat with onions, chili, and calamansi, and can be served as ulam or bar food.

Pancit

A dish that's influenced by Chinese culture and is often served at birthdays because noodles symbolize good health and a long life.

Lumpia

A flavorful snack or side dish that's evolved from Chinese spring rolls, and consists of a wrap made from flour or rice dough that's stuffed with meat and vegetables, coated with egg wash, and deep fried.
